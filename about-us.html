About Doughted.

Doughted. began with a simple thought: "Doughnuts are unhealthy."
Founded in 2024 by Erika Quizon and the rest of the Infinity9 team, our venture emerged from a shared love of doughnuts and a celebration of five years of friendship. We recognized that while sweets often satisfy cravings and alleviate stress, excessive indulgence can compromise one's health. So, many individuals just suppress their desire for sweets.
Doughted. aims to challenge this mindset by offering a healthier alternative. With a commitment to using real fruits and natural sweeteners, we provide guilt-free treats that cater to both taste and well-being.

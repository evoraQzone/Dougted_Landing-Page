Nutritional & Allergy Information.

Our products at Doughted. may contain or come into contact with common food allergens, including eggs, milk, soy, wheat, peanuts, and/or tree nuts. Despite our implementation of standard cleaning precautions, we cannot guarantee an allergen-free environment. We strongly advise exercising caution when consuming any of our products if you have allergies to any of the aforementioned ingredients.

Your safety and well-being are of utmost importance to us, and we encourage you to make informed choices based on your individual dietary needs. If you have any concerns or questions regarding allergens or gluten content, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.
